Experiences

Student Ambassador in Taiwan Region

Due to my profound enjoyment of the InvestIN Medical Camp experience, I was inspired to take on a more active role in promoting awareness of the program in Taiwan. Recognizing the limited familiarity in Taiwan, I went a step further and created an introductory video, hoping to inform more Taiwanese about the opportunities offered by InvestIN.

Intern, Physician Shadowing

Through this activity, I observed how doctors consulted patients, formulated treatment plans, resolved conflicts, and communicated with patients and other colleagues realistically. I also wrote a report on this experience. It also gives me an understanding of the life of a doctor as well as how the hospital operates.

Taiyang Long-term Care Corporation

Taiyang Long-term Care Corporation, a private long-term residential care, provides person-centered, empowering, and rehabilitative care services. It uses both additive and subtractive services, hoping the residents can return to their families.

In this volunteer activity, I was responsible for accompanying the elderly, helping them eat, reading newspapers, and doing other service projects. The specific things I do are to draw postcards with them, take them out for a walk, and do some simple crafts to accompany them. Moreover, I had the opportunity to communicate with the elders.
